## Why this is a voice calibration exemplar

The year-recap shows Auki's **celebration register** — warm, specific, named, playful. This is not the register the `aukiBuilderLens` enforces for diagnosis mode, but it shares DNA with it and teaches what Auki's tone range looks like.

The lens for Radiant outputs lives closer to the final paragraph of this recap than to its month-by-month celebration. But understanding the whole piece calibrates what warmth, playfulness, and named specificity look like in the Auki idiom — so that even in diagnosis mode, the voice isn't robotic.

## Why the integration is Trust

The essay's warmth + specificity + honest texture + consistent delivery of milestones produces the **Trust** emergent state (narrative + fairness integrated). Readers can see exactly what happened, who was involved, what's real versus what's in beta. That transparency builds confidence.

Hope is present at the close (long-horizon + fair distribution in the 2026 execution frame) but not dominant. Inspiration flashes in places (especially around "robot deployment becomes feasible at scale") but the piece is more delivery-receipt than future-rally.

## Key rhetorical moves

1. **Named-specificity pattern.** Every accomplishment is anchored in names/places/numbers. "7 digit ARR deal for Cactus." "Pepito in Bali." "500 builders." Abstractions are avoided.

2. **Character anecdotes as concrete illustrations.** "Terri got an understudy when Mech Jagger arrived. He's quite the dancer." Playfulness through characterization — Terri isn't "our robot," he's a named presence.

3. **Honest-texture mentions.** Things that didn't go perfectly are included, not elided. "The security team's radio frequency jammers made him lock up — literally." This honesty makes the wins more credible.

4. **The punchline move.** "— literally." "— but we're confident we're going to win." A rhythm break that lands a specific phrase after a structured sentence.

5. **Strategic closer.** The final paragraph compresses the whole year into one thesis: foundations → execution. List-becomes-argument.
